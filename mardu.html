This page will describe to you the uses of the Mardu deck in Magic: The Gathering.

The Mardu Houses are a 3 color clan on the plane of Tarkir. Their colors are Black, Red, and White.
Their unique mechanic in the game is called Raid. By attacking with a creature, and then casting the spell after (during Main Phase 2)
or after declaring attackers, an additional effect is had. This mechanic works on both Creatures and Sorceries. This leads to a very
aggressive, speedy style for Mardu decks, as they seek to crush their opponents in the first few turns.

Pros:
-Very speedy deck, if played correctly can win within the first few turns
-Can summon a large number of creatures to overwhelm the enemy
-Has direct damage, so it can get around beefy blockers


Cons:
- Is weak to boardwipes. If someone can do 1-2 damage to every creature, that usually will ruin you.
-No endgame power or massive, heavy hitters.
