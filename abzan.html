This page will describe to you the uses of the Abzan deck in Magic: The Gathering.

The Abzan Houses are a 3 color clan on the plane of Tarkir. Their colors are Black, Green, and White.
Their unique mechanic in the game is called Outlast. By paying some mana and tapping a creature, they will gain a +1/+1 counter.
This ability can be used to scale very weak creatures into much stronger ones given enough time. Since the costs to activate the ability
are usually very cheap, you can use an ability every turn to make your board presence that much stronger.

Pros:
- Can scale cheap creatures into heavy threats over time.
- All creatures benefit each other with their abilities
- Creatures have cheap costs, and their Outlast costs are cheap as well.

Cons:
- Is very weak to targeted removal.
- Takes a few turns to set up all of its Creatures and Enchantments.
- Has very little removal itself, and so other people can set up faster than it.
