This page will describe to you the uses of the Jeskai deck in Magic: The Gathering.

The Jeskai Way are a 3 color clan on the plane of Tarkir. Their colors are Blue, Red, and White.

Their unique mechanic in the game is called Prowess. This ability is only placed upon creatures. After casting a non-creature spell (i.e. Sorceries, Instants, Enchantments, and Artifacts), the Creature gets +1/+1 until end of turn. This leads to a very tempo-based playstyle, where weak creatures can become very strong after casting a few spells.

Pros:
- Can deal great amounts of damage via combat tricks - cast many cheap instants when an opponent lets a creature by unblocked, and a seemingly small creature will turn into a massive one.
- Can control the flow of the game through spells that control their opponents and their creatures.


Cons:
-Jeskai decks are very reliant upon drawing the right cards. If they don't get useful cards to alter the game, they tend to be very weak.
- They need creatures on the field to effectively cast spells and utilize Prowess. Without creatures, their spells are generally ineffective.
