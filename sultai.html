This page will describe to you the uses of the Sultai deck in Magic: The Gathering.

The Sultai Brood is a 3 color clan on the plane of Tarkir. Their colors are Black, Green, and Blue.
Their unique mechanic in the game is called Delve. Whenever they cast a spell with the mechanic Delve, they can exile any number of cards from their graveyard,
and the card will be that much cheaper. This ability only lowers the cost of colorless mana, however. This ability can turn very expensive cards into cheap ones.
By using cards that mill themselves out, the Sultai can end up then casting many spells for low amounts of mana - it turns your graveyard into another source of mana.

Pros:
- Can summon large creatures for cheap costs.
- Tends to control the late game with massive, game changing spells.
- Has targeted destruction to remove threats
Cons:
- A very slow deck - It takes a long time to get it's engine going.
- Somewhat low creature base.
- Without a graveyard, their creatures are very expensive.
