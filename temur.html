This page will describe to you the uses of the Temur deck in Magic: The Gathering.

The Temur Frontier is a 3 color clan on the plane of Tarkir. Their colors are Red, Green, and Blue.
Their unique mechanic in the game is called Ferocity. When the spell is cast, if you control a creature with power 4 or greater, then an additional effect happens.
Many of the Temur creatures have a large power, so their mechanic will often times be procced. Temur decks also have a good deal of Mana Ramp, meaning that
they can summon their creatures early, and can consistently cast them since they will have

Pros:
- Has many cheap, large creatures.
- Has good mana ramp to summon creatures early.
- Has targeted removal and destruction to remove the smaller threats to their large creatures.
Cons:
- Weak to large hordes of creatures, as they have few.
- It centers around ramp aspect of the deck - without mana, it cannot cast the proper spells.
- Weak early game - requires ramp to survive.
